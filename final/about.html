About Us

Fresh Fruits And Produce Since 1995

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1995 by John Smith, a local farmer who saw a need for fresh, locally-grown produce in the Carlsbad area. Starting with just a few acres of farmland, John and his family worked tirelessly to build the business, planting and harvesting a variety of fruits and vegetables throughout the year.
